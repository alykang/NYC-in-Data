Atlantic Avenue: The street with the most vehicle crashes (as of 2024)

Driving in New York City is no easy activity. Tight roads, large groups of pedestrians, speedy cyclists, aggressive drivers, and deafening honks. NYC is a dangerous place to drive. Brooklyn in particular proves to be the most crash-prone borough to drive in. In fact, according to Forbes, Brooklyn has the highest percentage of accidents out of all NYC boroughs. But where exactly do the majority of these crashes occur? And why? Below you will find the top 5 intersections in Brooklyn where the most amount of crashes have occurred in the past 10 years based on NYPD live data.
Atlantic Avenue from the Brooklyn Docks to Gateway Park at Van Wyck Expressway is 10.3 miles long, with 7.4 miles in Brooklyn, making it one of Brooklyn's longest streets. Out of all motor vehicles that have been recorded in Brooklyn, 9,977 of them have occurred on Atlantic Avenue.
